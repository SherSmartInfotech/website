Joomla:

Why Select Joomla? Because, why not? One of the most popular CMS available in the market, Joomla certainly ranks high at the moment. Content Management Systems are often referred to as CMS. PHP language is the core of Joomla!, hence it can work proficiently with the PHP based websites. More than 6,000 free and commercial extensions are available in the Joomla! Extension Directory. Joomla! Extensions can be used directly or integrated into other systems to generate advance system. The three time awardee of the Packt Publishing Open Source Content Management System, Joomla! is highly influential in the CMS industry.

Is it feasible to work with Joomla? The answer is YES. Joomla! can be installed with any trouble since many web hosts provide support for Joomla! web application. As the installation is simple and hassle-free, it is less worrisome for the organization. Joomla! extensions can be plug-ins, components, modules, templates and languages. To track the components of the website is part of the content management. Joomla! will handle simple text, photos, audio, videos and all other important content. Joomla! CMS can be used for the large scale enterprise or the individuals or non-profit organizations.
