Cross Platform Development:

Cross platform technology is used to create a mobile application that can work on different mobile operating systems. From the business viewpoint, it is beneficial since it has same frame of the application but different structure to deploy on multiple platforms. To generate more business revenue, it is essential that the application reached to the mass audience and Cross Platform technology can help you achieve that.

Consider the cellphone device platforms for which you wish to alter the app using the cross platform technology. E.g. you have an android application and now want to use for the iOS application. Cross-platform technology gives you the freedom to be all-inclusive or selective as you deem appropriate. One caution to follow is to retain the final source code as the company property while choosing the cross-platform development. It is essential to sign the Non-Disclosure Agreement by both the parties, including the source code. This specifies that the development company is legally striped of their rights over the cross platform development once the project is over. Since the client has already paid for the services, it is only fair to hand over complete rights to them as the owner.
